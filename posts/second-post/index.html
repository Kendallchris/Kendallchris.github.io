Understanding Geometry with C++: Three Points Analysis

In this blog post, I’ll discuss a fascinating C++ project that involves geometric analysis. The project, titled “Lab 1 - threepoints.cpp,” is designed to read three points and determine whether they form a line or a triangle. This is an excellent example of applying mathematical concepts in programming.

Project Overview

The program takes three points as input and uses various geometric formulas to analyze their arrangement. If the points form a straight line, it calculates the slope and length of the line. If they form a triangle, it computes each side’s length, the angles in radians and degrees, the perimeter, and the area of the triangle.
